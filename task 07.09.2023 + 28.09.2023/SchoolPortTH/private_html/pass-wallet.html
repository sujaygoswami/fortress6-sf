Now, save Stephan Bulawa's digital business card from School Portraits (Thailand) Co., Ltd. to your Wallet app (Apple Wallet / Google Wallet).

  1. Tap on "Add to Apple Wallet"

  2. The card is displayed in a preview.

  3. Save the card in Apple Wallet
    By clicking 'Add,' the card will be saved in your Wallet app.

  4. Check the Wallet app on your iPhone
    Open your Wallet app to confirm if the card has been saved.
    Each Wallet pass has a backside that you can access by tapping on the three dots in the upper right corner of your iPhone screen.

  1. Android user? Click here to save!

    Tap on „Add to Google Wallet“
    To store the card in Google Wallet, select the 'Add to Google Wallet' button. This action will open a page where you can save the pass to your Google Wallet

  2. Save in Google Wallet
    You must be signed in to your Google account to store the card. Ensure it's the same account you're using for Google Wallet. After selecting 'Add to Google Wallet,' a preview page will appear. Tap on 'Add‘.

  3. Show the pass in the Google Wallet App
    "After clicking ‚Add,‘ open Google Wallet or click „View in Wallet“. Your card will appear below your other cards in the app."
