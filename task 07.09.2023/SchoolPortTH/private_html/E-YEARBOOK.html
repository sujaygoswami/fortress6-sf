    The Future of the Yearbook
    Everywhere, anytime on any device

We offer your school the solution to publish your Yearbook on your school homepage with our comfortable viewer software. We can publish your Yearbook with a ISBN number in any book store. Additional we can publish your e-yearbook at the Shops of Amazon, Apple, Google & Co.

Your benefits:

- We do everything to publish
- Your yearbook can order in every book store via the ISBN number
- Perfekt supplement for a printed yearbook
- Perfekt for a school anniversary

How to incorporate the Yearbook on the school homepage:

We pixel the student faces and transform your yearbook from a PDF in a e-Yearbook and we will send you a HTML-Code. The HTML-Code is easy to incorporate on your school homepage.
