Schools of all academic levels and sizes use our ID card solutions in a variety of ways. Some schools simply require basic photo IDs, while others integrate these ID cards with applications like meal plan programs, library and equipment checkout, time and attendance, cashless payment, and even access control for campus buildings and student housing.

Some applications of ID Cards by schools include the following :

• Access Control across your entire campus to track who's coming and going
• Registration processes and recordkeeping
• Meal programs utilizing bar codes to keep individual students lunch plan programs confidential from other students
• Library and Equipment check-in/check-out
• Activity passes with fun, colorful tickets promoting plays, dances and sporting events
• Fundraising activities with printed coupons or punch-out cards for use at participating area merchants.
