Die Google Wallet App bietet die bestmöglichste Benutzererfahrung. Google Wallet Karten werden auf Servern gespeichert und über das Google-Konto der Nutzer*innen auf allen Geräten synchronisiert, die die Google Wallet App installiert haben.

2) Die Karte öffnen.

3) Nach dem Öffnen der Karte wird eine Vorschau der Karte angezeigt. Erst ein Klick auf “Zu Wallet hinzufügen” speichert die Karte in der Wallet-App.

The Google Wallet app offers the best user experience. Google Wallet cards are stored on servers and synchronized across users' Google accounts on all devices where the Google Wallet app is installed. Alternatively, you can also use the PassWallet app, although we cannot automatically update student IDs here. However, you can trigger an update manually.

If you cannot download the pass using the buttons above or if the buttons do not display correctly, you can download the file via this link:'
Download student ID card
2) Open the map.

3) After opening the map, a preview of the map will be displayed. Only by clicking on “Add to Wallet” will the card be saved in the wallet app.

4) Check the wallet app on your smartphone.

Whenever the wallet app is opened, the new digital student ID card can be displayed. The back of the student ID card can be opened by clicking on the three dots.

Manual deletion of your data:
Alternatively, you have the option of having your data for the digital student ID deleted at any time. Please note that you will no longer be able to use this service in the future. Please write to us with your customer number datenschutz@energy-imaging.de
